What Can We Do To Prevent Diseases Caused by Air Pollution?


Reduce Car Emissions

  • Use public transportation, carpool, bike, or walk instead of driving alone.

  • Maintain vehicles to ensure they are running efficiently and emitting fewer pollutants.

  • Consider switching to electric or hybrid vehicles.

Support Green Initiatives

  • Advocate for and support policies aimed at reducing emissions from industrial sources and promoting clean energy.

  • Participate in community efforts to plant trees and create green spaces, which can help improve air quality.

Stay Informed

  • Regularly check the Air Quality Index (AQI) to stay informed about the air quality in your area.

  • Apps and websites provide real-time updates and can help you plan your activities to avoid high pollution times.

Use Mask

  • Wearing masks, especially N95 or similar quality masks, can filter out harmful particles like PM2.5 from the air. This is particularly important on days with high pollution levels or when engaging in outdoor activities.
